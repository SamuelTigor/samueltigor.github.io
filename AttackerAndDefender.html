Attacker

In Rainbow Six Siege, attackers are the operatives tasked with penetrating the defensive positions set up by the opposing team to complete various objectives. Each attacker brings a unique set of skills, weapons, and gadgets to the table, allowing for diverse strategies and playstyles. Here's a breakdown of some key aspects of Rainbow Six Siege attackers:


Objectives: Attackers are typically tasked with objectives such as rescuing hostages, defusing bombs, or securing an area. The specific objectives vary depending on the game mode being played.

Defender

In Rainbow Six Siege, defenders are the operatives tasked with fortifying their position and repelling the attackers' attempts to breach and complete the objectives. Here's an overview of the key aspects of Rainbow Six Siege defenders:

Objectives: Defenders aim to prevent attackers from completing their objectives, which may include protecting hostages, guarding bombs, or securing an area. The defenders must hold their ground and thwart the attackers' advances to win the round.
